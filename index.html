About us

West gates & security is a south-wales based security business owned and ran by Jason Thomas. We are primarily situated in Pembrokeshire, but work throughout the United Kingdom specialising in the Design, Installation, Maintenance and Reparation of Security and Networking equipment.

West gates & security hold various industry recognised accreditations, and work to ensure our operations and installations are the highest standards possible. We set high standards for our work, and we are happy to work with customers to fulfill their specialised business requirements.

Automatic gates & barriers

West Gates & Security can Install, maintain and repair an extensive range of automatic gates and barriers to suit your needs in domestic, commercial or industrial environments. Gates and barriers can be a valuable solution to control or restrict access to your private or business premises from unwanted visitors or intruders while allowing authorised staff and guest access whenever they are authorised to do so.

Gates on domestic premises add a layer of protection for your home as intruders would need to make a conscious effort to gain access to your property and the intruder is likely to look elsewhere.

Fibre optics & Networking

Fibre optic cables offer a range of benefits that go far beyond what is expected from traditional copper cables. Fibre optics are capable of transmitting data at a much higher bandwidth, as well as carrying signals at speeds that are about 30% slower than the speed of light. They are also sturdier, much more reliable, and can carry signals at much longer distances than copper cables.

The true value of fibre optic installation is in the future-proofing of your business. They serve as an ideal method of protecting information security, and can vastly increase productivity via eliminating speed issues that may arise.

CCTV & intruder Alarms

West Gates & Security can design, install, maintain and repair any type of CCTV system, to suit your needs and requirements. CCTV alone is a massive deterrent and often prevents crime before it occurs. It is useful for preventing and detecting criminal activity as well as monitoring the safety and wellbeing of people and property.

Intruder alarms are the basis of most building security systems and are fitted as standard in almost all commercial and industrial premises for good reason. The cost to have an Intruder alarm installed can be a fraction of the cost to replace damaged or stolen property or items from your home or business

Access
Control

West Gates & Security can Install, maintain and repair a large range of Access Control systems to suit your needs. From a single door keypad entry system to a large commercial or industrial premises consisting of swipe, proximity or biometric technology with detailed access permissions and time management.

Access control systems, when installed to sufficient specification, can also be used for staff attendance recording and mustering. Mustering is the term used to track locations and numbers of staff or visitors within a building or within an area of a building. This is especially useful when Fire alarm or site evacuation emergencies occur.

Intercom Systems

Intercom systems are an effective and essential system for efficiently communicating with employees across the total length of your office space or commercial property. West gates & security can install intercom systems to assist with communication, supplement your existing cctv system, and offer augmented secure access to your facility.

WIth an intercom system, you can also restrict access to certain areas of your commercial property, and deter criminals and loiterers from attempting to enter without authorisation. Truly instant communication within your office space is possible, allowing for vastly increased productivity.
